International Conference on

AI for Sustainable, Inclusive & Responsible Futures

(AI-SIRF 2026)

8 – 10 October 2026 | CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Pune Lavasa Campus | Multidisciplinary Innovation

About the Conference

AI-SIRF 2026 brings together global researchers, industry experts, and academicians to explore the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in building a sustainable, inclusive, and responsible future. The conference emphasizes multidisciplinary innovation that drives ethical AI adoption, environmental resilience, and social empowerment through technology.
